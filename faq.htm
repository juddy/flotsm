Q: OK... But what /is/ this, really?
A: flostm is 'flotsam', made somewhat tangible. Information, metadata, data, all collected into a "cardlike" form. Something like a trading card, a barcode (or a permutaion thereof), a drink coaster, a Portable Document Format file. Flotsm.com's produce /is/ what it represents in a way that trading cards cannot.

Q: Can I build sets of cards representing all the favorite things in my life?
A: Yes, in fact, that's one of the intended uses of flotsm. DIY trading cards. Make a game, create a character attribute/dataset template and submit it to me. I need some working models, and you'll get credit. Please, no copyrighted or existing/fan works!
